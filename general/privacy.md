## 3. Cookies and tracking

This website does not set cookies and does not use any tracking, analytics, or marketing tools. No information is stored on or read from your device, so no consent under § 25 TDDDG is required.

All fonts, scripts, and images are served from this website's own hosting. Visiting the pages does not trigger requests to third-party font or script providers (such as Google Fonts or content delivery networks).

## 4. Embedded video

One course page embeds a single YouTube video using YouTube's privacy-enhanced mode (youtube-nocookie.com), provided by Google Ireland Limited, Gordon House, Barrow Street, Dublin 4, Ireland. In this mode, YouTube does not store cookies on your device unless you actively start playback. When the page containing the video is opened, your browser establishes a technical connection to YouTube's servers to load the player, which transmits your IP address. If you play the video, YouTube may process further usage data under its own privacy policy ([policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)).

**Legal basis:** Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in presenting educational video content); any processing following playback is subject to Google's responsibility.

## 5. Your data protection rights

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Right to access (Art. 15 GDPR)**
- **Right to rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)**
- **Right to erasure (Art. 17 GDPR)**
- **Right to restrict processing (Art. 18 GDPR)**
- **Right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)**
- **Right to object (Art. 21 GDPR)**

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us as the controller using the details in Section 1. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority, in particular in the EU member state of your habitual residence, place of work, or the place of the alleged infringement.
